## Method 2: Convert JPG to PDF on Windows

Windows operating system allows JPG to PDF conversion with built-in features:

1. Select the images you want to convert
2. Right-click and select "Print"
3. Choose "Microsoft Print to PDF" as the printer
4. Adjust paper size and layout
5. Click "Print" and save your PDF

This method is completely free and requires no additional software.

## Method 3: Convert JPG to PDF on Mac

For Mac users, the Preview app provides an excellent solution:

1. Open your images in Preview
2. Go to "File" > "Export as PDF"
3. Choose the filename and save location
4. Click "Save"

To combine multiple images into one PDF, select all images at once, open them in Preview, then use "File" > "Export as PDF".

## JPG to PDF Conversion Tips

### For Best Results:
- **Use high-resolution images** (at least 300 DPI)
- **Choose uniform image sizes** for consistent PDF appearance
- **Use PNG** for black and white documents instead of JPEG

### Common Issues and Solutions:
- **Large file size**: Compress images first, then convert to PDF
- **Wrong orientation**: Rotate images before converting
- **Quality loss**: Use high-quality JPEG (%90+ quality)
