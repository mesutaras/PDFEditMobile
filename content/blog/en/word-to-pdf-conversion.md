Converting Word documents to PDF is the most effective way to preserve formatting and ensure consistent appearance across all devices.

## Why Convert Word to PDF?

- Fonts and layout remain the same on every device
- Documents are protected against unauthorized editing
- Ideal for professional sharing

## Method 2: Convert with Microsoft Word

1. Open your Word document
2. Go to "File" > "Save As"
3. Select "PDF" as file type
4. Click "Save"

## Method 3: Convert with Google Docs

1. Upload your Word file to Google Drive
2. Open with Google Docs
3. Go to "File" > "Download" > "PDF"
