Saving PDF pages as images is ideal for using documents in presentations, publishing on websites, or sharing on social media. PDF to JPG conversion lets you save each page as a separate image file.

## Why Convert PDF to JPG?

- To use PDF pages as visuals in presentations
- To publish PDF documents on your website
- To share PDF pages on social media
- To use PDF content in image editing software

## Method 2: Convert PDF to JPG with Adobe Acrobat

1. Open your PDF in Adobe Acrobat
2. Go to "File" > "Export To" > "Image" > "JPEG"
3. Configure resolution settings
4. Click "Save"

## Method 3: Convert with Preview on Mac

1. Open PDF in Preview app
2. Go to "File" > "Export"
3. Select "JPEG" as format
4. Adjust quality and save

## Tips

- **PNG format** is better for text-heavy pages
