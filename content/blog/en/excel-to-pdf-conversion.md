## Why Convert Excel to PDF?
- Table formatting stays the same on every device
- Formulas and calculations are preserved
- Ideal for professional report sharing

## Method 2: Convert with Microsoft Excel
1. Open your Excel file
2. Go to "File" > "Save As" > "PDF"
3. Click "Save"

## Method 3: Convert with Google Sheets
1. Upload your Excel file to Google Drive
2. Open with Google Sheets
3. Go to "File" > "Download" > "PDF"
