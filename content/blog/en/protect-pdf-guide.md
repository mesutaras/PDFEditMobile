Protect your sensitive PDF documents from unauthorized access with password encryption.

## Method 1: Protect with PDFEditMobile
1. **PDFEditMobile.com** > "Protect PDF"
2. Upload PDF > Set password > Configure permissions
3. Click "Protect" > Download
